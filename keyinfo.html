Introduction to Policies

For the Federation to run effectively it is so important for staff, students,
parent/guardians and governors to be absolutely clear about how systems
operate and understand how the Federation and schools within it, respond
to given situations.


With this in mind, all statutory policies are published on all of our
websites, plus some voluntary policies we feel are good practice. The full
list of published policies in the Federation/school can be seen on the
policy document page.



We would encourage parents/guardians and friends of the Federation/school to
read our policies and familiarise themselves with the Federation’s
procedures and protocols.
